✅ Adults 50+ — regardless of prior shingles or prior vaccine.

✅ Adults 18+ who are immunocompromised: cancer on chemotherapy, organ transplant, immunosuppressive medication, certain autoimmune & haematologic conditions, other causes of weakened immunity.

Dosing: 2 doses; second 2–6 months after first (1–2 months for some immunocompromised).
